Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 28, 2026

Overview

Tunebar is a menu bar music player for macOS. Your privacy is important to us, and Tunebar is designed to keep your data on your device.

Data Collection

Tunebar does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All your music files and metadata remain entirely on your device.

Network Requests

When you use the "Fetch Metadata" feature, Tunebar sends the track title and artist name to the MusicBrainz API to look up metadata. No personal or device information is included in these requests. No other network requests are made.

Analytics & Tracking

Tunebar does not include any analytics, crash reporting, or tracking frameworks.

Third-Party Services

The only third-party service used is the MusicBrainz public API, solely for optional metadata lookups. Please refer to the MusicBrainz Privacy Policy for their data practices.
