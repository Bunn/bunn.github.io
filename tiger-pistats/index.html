Introducing Pi Stats for Mac OS X Tiger

Pi Stats for Mac OS X Tiger, the top-notch solution for managing and visualizing pi-hole data on the most advanced operating system, Mac OS X Tiger. With its cool brushed metal design and smooth interface, this powerful tool puts you in control of your pi-hole setup.

Enable or disable it with a simple click, and stay informed with real-time updates on its status, blocked content percentage, total queries, queries blocked, and blocklist number.

Supported version: Mac OS X 10.4 (Universal - PPC and Intel)

Note: Pi Stats for Mac OS Tiger is a project made just for fun. I have no intentions of adding more features. This project is offered at a "pay what you want" price, so please take this into consideration. That said, I'd appreciate any amount you're willing to pay :)
